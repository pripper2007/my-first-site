About

Building the future
of digital payments

Pedro Ripper is co-founder and CEO of Bemobi (BMOB3), a Brazilian public technology company with a global footprint spanning more than 50 countries. He has led the company's journey from its origins in mobile services to its current position as a digital payments and software platform for large enterprises.

Prior to co-founding Bemobi, Pedro held senior executive roles at Promon, Cisco Systems, and Oi. He holds a degree in Computer Engineering and a Master's in Software Engineering from PUC-Rio, and also completed executive education programs at Harvard Business School and Singularity University.

Pedro is also a co-founder and investor in other technology ventures and has served on the boards of leading Brazilian companies.
