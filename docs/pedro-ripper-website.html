Pedro Ripper is a visionary entrepreneur and technology leader with a proven track record of building scalable, global companies. As co-founder and CEO of Bemobi, he has transformed the mobile technology landscape, establishing one of Brazil's most successful tech companies with operations spanning four continents.

Throughout his 20+ years in the technology sector, Pedro has consistently demonstrated an ability to identify emerging opportunities, build high-performing teams, and create sustainable business models. His leadership philosophy centers on innovation, operational excellence, and creating lasting value for stakeholders.
